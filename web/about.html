Purpose

Groceries should fit your budget before you reach the checkout.

Grocery Planner helps people turn a weekly budget into a practical shopping list, compare nearby store estimates, and choose a smarter route before spending time, gas, or money.

Mission

Make food planning clearer, cheaper, and less stressful.

The site is built for shoppers who want quick answers: what to buy, where prices look better, what meals those groceries can become, and whether extra stops are worth it.

How it helps

It turns scattered grocery decisions into one plan.

  • Builds a list around your budget, item count, preferences, and health goals.
  • Shows potential savings against higher nearby store estimates.
  • Recommends a compact route instead of sending you to every store.
  • Lets The Chef suggest meals from the groceries already in your plan.

What to expect

Use it as a planning assistant, not a final receipt.

Prices are estimates unless you refresh live pricing. The value is speed and direction: spend less time comparing stores manually and more time shopping with a clear list.
